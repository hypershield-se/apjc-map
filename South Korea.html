Autonomous Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
PIPA Mandates data segmentation and other measures to protect personal information. Hypershield segments sensitive systems to ensure compliance with PIPA.
ICNA Requires network segregation to prevent cyberattacks and protect communications. Hypershield dynamically segments networks to isolate and protect critical assets.
Cybersecurity Framework for Critical Infrastructure Mandates segmentation of critical infrastructure systems to reduce cyber risks. Hypershield isolates critical systems using automated segmentation techniques.

Distributed Exploit Protection

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
PIPA Requires measures to prevent unauthorized access and breaches of personal data. Hypershield detects and neutralizes exploit-based attacks targeting personal data.
ICNA Mandates intrusion prevention to protect communications networks. Hypershield provides exploit protection to prevent intrusions and attacks on critical systems.
National Cybersecurity Strategy Focuses on real-time detection and response to cyber threats. Hypershield provides distributed exploit protection to prevent lateral movement of threats.

L4 Zone Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
Cybersecurity Framework for Critical Infrastructure Mandates isolation of critical systems to reduce risks of cyberattacks. Hypershield enforces L4 segmentation to isolate and secure critical infrastructure systems.
PIPA Requires strict isolation of personal data systems to ensure security. Hypershield uses L4 segmentation to protect personal data from unauthorized access.
ISO/IEC 27001 Recommends segmenting systems based on risk profiles to minimize vulnerabilities. Hypershield enforces segmentation to align with ISO/IEC 27001 requirements.
