Autonomous Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
Essential Eight Section 4.1 mandates application control to restrict unauthorized applications. Hypershield enforces segmentation to isolate unauthorized traffic and prevent misuse.
SOCI Act Part 2A requires segmentation to protect Critical Infrastructure (CI) systems. Hypershield dynamically segments CI systems to ensure compliance with SOCI requirements.
ASD Guidelines Recommends segmenting sensitive systems to reduce cyber risks. Hypershield isolates high-risk systems, ensuring safe operation and compliance.
AESCSF Mandates separation of Operational Technology (OT) and IT systems in the energy sector. Hypershield enforces segmentation to isolate OT and IT systems.

Distributed Exploit Protection

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
Essential Eight Section 4.3 mandates timely patching to reduce exploit risks. Hypershield detects and mitigates exploit attempts on unpatched systems.
Privacy Act Section 13G requires organizations to prevent unauthorized access to personal data. Hypershield blocks exploits targeting personal data systems.
SOCI Act Part 2C mandates real-time monitoring and incident response for CI systems. Hypershield provides exploit detection and response capabilities for CI compliance.

L4 Zone Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
SOCI Act Part 2A requires isolation of critical systems through segmentation. Hypershield enforces L4 segmentation to isolate sensitive CI systems.
Privacy Act Part IIIA mandates secure segmentation of systems handling personal data. Hypershield isolates personal data environments to prevent unauthorized access.
ISO/IEC 27001 Recommends segmenting systems based on risk profiles. Hypershield enforces granular segmentation for high-risk systems.
