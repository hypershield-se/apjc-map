Autonomous Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
EIT Law Mandates measures to protect sensitive electronic data and ensure system integrity. Hypershield segments sensitive systems, minimizing risks of unauthorized access or breaches.
Government Regulation No. 71/2019 Requires data protection and network isolation for critical infrastructure systems. Hypershield enforces segmentation to secure critical infrastructure systems.
MR 4/2021 Provides guidelines for segmenting public and private sector systems to reduce cyber risks. Hypershield dynamically segments systems to isolate and protect critical assets.

Distributed Exploit Protection

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
Presidential Regulation No. 82/2022 Mandates incident prevention and response for critical systems. Hypershield provides exploit prevention capabilities to protect critical infrastructure.
GR 71/2019 Requires measures to prevent service disruptions in critical systems. Hypershield detects and neutralizes exploit-based attacks targeting critical systems.
EIT Law Requires measures to prevent unauthorized access to sensitive systems. Hypershield provides real-time exploit detection and prevention capabilities.

L4 Zone Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
MR 4/2021 Mandates the use of segmentation to protect public and private sector systems. Hypershield enforces L4 segmentation to isolate critical systems and reduce attack surfaces.
ISO/IEC 27001 Recommends segmenting systems based on risk profiles to minimize vulnerabilities. Hypershield enforces segmentation to align with ISO/IEC 27001 requirements.
Presidential Regulation No. 82/2022 Requires isolation of critical systems to reduce risks of cyberattacks. Hypershield enforces L4 segmentation to secure critical infrastructure systems.
