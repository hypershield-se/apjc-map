Autonomous Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
PDPA 2010 Mandates protection of personal data against unauthorized or accidental access. Hypershield segments sensitive data environments to minimize unauthorized access risks.
Cyber Security Strategy 2020-2024 Focuses on securing critical national infrastructure and improving risk management. Hypershield dynamically segments critical systems to isolate and protect them.
CNII Guidelines Mandates the protection of critical systems in finance, energy, and healthcare sectors. Hypershield provides automated segmentation to secure critical national systems.

Distributed Exploit Protection

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
PDPA 2010 Requires the prevention of breaches that could expose sensitive personal data. Hypershield detects and neutralizes exploit-based attacks targeting sensitive data.
Cyber Security Strategy 2020-2024 Emphasizes advanced threat detection and response capabilities for critical infrastructure. Hypershield provides distributed exploit protection to prevent cyberattacks on critical systems.
CMA 1998 Mandates securing communication networks and preventing service disruptions. Hypershield prevents exploit-based attacks on communication networks and ensures service continuity.

L4 Zone Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
Cyber Security Strategy 2020-2024 Promotes isolating critical systems to reduce the impact of cyber incidents. Hypershield enforces L4 segmentation to isolate critical systems and reduce attack surfaces.
CNII Guidelines Requires segmentation of critical national systems to prevent unauthorized access. Hypershield uses L4 segmentation to secure critical national infrastructure systems.
ISO/IEC 27001 Recommends segmenting systems based on risk profiles to minimize security vulnerabilities. Hypershield enforces granular segmentation to align with ISO/IEC 27001 requirements.
