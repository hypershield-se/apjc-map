Autonomous Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
Cybersecurity Basic Act Emphasizes protecting critical infrastructure and government systems. Hypershield dynamically segments critical systems to prevent unauthorized access.
FSA Guidelines for Financial Institutions Mandates the use of network segmentation to protect financial systems. Hypershield provides advanced segmentation to isolate financial systems and reduce risks.
NISC Cybersecurity Policy Requires the protection of critical infrastructure systems through risk-based segmentation. Hypershield isolates critical assets using automated segmentation based on risk profiles.

Distributed Exploit Protection

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
Cybersecurity Basic Act Highlights the need for real-time monitoring and response to cyber threats. Hypershield provides exploit detection and protection for critical systems.
FSA Guidelines for Financial Institutions Mandates the detection and prevention of exploit-based attacks on financial networks. Hypershield provides distributed exploit protection to neutralize cyber threats in financial systems.

L4 Zone Segmentation

Regulation Relevant Sections How Hypershield Aligns
Cybersecurity Basic Act Requires isolation of critical systems to reduce the risk of cyberattacks. Hypershield enforces L4 segmentation to isolate critical systems and protect sensitive data.
APPI Mandates protecting personal data from breaches and unauthorized access. Hypershield enforces L4 segmentation to restrict unauthorized access to sensitive data environments.
ISO/IEC 27001 Recommends segmenting systems based on risk profiles to minimize vulnerabilities. Hypershield enforces granular segmentation to align with ISO/IEC 27001 standards.
